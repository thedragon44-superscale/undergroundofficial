📖 Quick Start & Security Guide (Tap to Expand)
1. Total Encryption: StreetBook utilizes True End-to-End Encryption (E2EE) built directly into your browser via WebCrypto. Your messages are encrypted before they leave your device using a master key locked by your Vault Password. The central servers literally cannot read your Private Messages or Gang Chats. Ever.

2. Invite Ledger: The network is closed. You must use the Terminal below to mint single-use registration keys. You are mathematically linked to the operators you invite. If they act out of line, your node reputation is impacted.

3. Financial Anonymity: The Financial Vault runs on the Bitcoin Lightning Network. You can instantly route funds to other operators using the "Direct Transfer" button, or request funds anonymously inside chat channels via the Escrow feature.

Note on Vault Generation: Vault generation is instant upon registration. If your Vault says "Offline", your server API keys were missing during your account creation. You must mint an invite key, log out, and create a fresh profile to activate it.

4. The Global Feed: Everything on the Feed tab is public to the network. Use Private Messages for sensitive intel.

📲 Install Native App

Install StreetBook directly to your home screen for fullscreen native app access without the browser URL bar.

🍎 iOS Apple Instructions:
Apple strictly blocks automatic app installations. To install StreetBook on your iPhone or iPad:
1. Open this page in the Safari browser.
2. Tap the Share Icon (the square with the arrow pointing up at the bottom of your screen).
3. Scroll down the list and tap "Add to Home Screen".
4. Tap "Add" in the top right corner.
